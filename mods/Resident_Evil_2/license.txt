If you're a fan of Resident Evil 2 and want to add a dark touch to your browsing
experience, it might be worth checking out a mod that customizes your Opera GX 
browser. This mod includes classic menu sounds from Resident Evil 2 and a Raccoon
City Police Department (RPD) wallpaper to give your browser a sleek and spooky look.

After installing the mod, the Resident Evil 2 menu sound will replace the default 
browser sounds, allowing you to feel immersed in the game even while surfing the web. 
In addition, custom RPD wallpaper will decorate your browser's home page, giving you
the feeling of being inside the game.

With the mod installed, you can enjoy a unique and personalized browsing 
experience that reflects your passion for the classic horror game. 
Enjoy the sounds and aesthetics of Resident Evil 2 while surfing 
the web with Opera GX!
